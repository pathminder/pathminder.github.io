---
title: "*time shudder*"
sources:
  - Pathfinder Roleplaying Game Advanced Class Guide
school: transmutation
spell_lists:
  - {name: magus, level: 2}
  - {name: sorcerer/wizard, level: 2}
  - {name: summoner, level: 2}
casting_time: 1 standard action
components:
  - V
  - S
  - M (a tiny hourglass)
range: 30 ft.
area: 30-ft.-radius emanation centered on you
duration: 1 round
saving_throw: none
spell_resistance: yes
---

You cause a ripple in time that affects all creatures in the area. At the start of each creature's turn, there is a 50% chance the creature is hasted (as [*haste*](/spells/haste/)); otherwise it is slowed (as [*slow*](/spells/slow/)).
