The target exudes stinking vapors that fill its space. The vapors do not obscure sight, but it nauseates creatures as [*stinking cloud*](/spells/stinking-cloud/). The target is immune to these vapors. Each creature that enters or ends its turn in the area filled with these vapors must succeed at a Fortitude save or become nauseated for as long as it remains in the square and for 1d4+1 rounds after it leaves.
